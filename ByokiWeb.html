◆  The Manifesto

We Don't Follow.
We Lead.

Byoki was born from the South African streets. The streets at 3am when everything's honest. We took the word for sick and made it a crown.

Born Sad is not a contradiction. It's the only honest design language for those who've seen both sides — the bottom and the top — and chose to own both.

"Born Sad. Crossed it out.
Died Lit. That's the story."

Each piece is a chapter. Baroque cherubs carrying gold. Graffiti over scripture. Beauty that doesn't apologise for its scars.
